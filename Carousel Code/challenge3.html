Hanah's Track and Field

I run Varsity Track and Field for Howard University. Even though I was never one of those athletes who tried to make sports an integral part of my life, it has in fact become part of my life. Playing a sport as a D1 athlete, even if it is at a largely academiv school, becomes like a second full time job. As the saying goes, track is life.
